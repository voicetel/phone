Features

Everything you need to place and receive high-quality calls, plus platform-specific advantages.

Common across all platforms
  • ✔️ Secure SIP over TLS
  • ✔️ DTMF (RFC 2833 and SIP INFO)
  • ✔️ Caller ID, privacy toggle, and history
  • ✔️ NAT-friendly ICE/STUN configuration
  • ✔️ Config persistence with local storage
  • ✔️ Troubleshooting log and diagnostics
Platform-specific
  • Browser mic permissions guide
  • Instant launch links (tel: / custom scheme)
  • No install — perfect for kiosks & VDI
  • System tray & global mute hotkey
  • Auto-start on login, auto-register
  • Crash-resilient reconnect & logs
  • Native audio routing & Bluetooth
  • Background mode with quick actions
